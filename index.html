Hello!

My name is Will Altman, and I am a second year Computer Science and Engineering student at The Ohio State University.

I am an active trumpet player in TBDBITLThe Ohio State University Marching Band, also known as The Best Damn Band In The Land and several other campus ensembles, and in my free time I enjoy writing about movie scenes I find interesting.
